Still have questions? We have answers.

Goals & Reporting in Asana is a unified capability set that lets you connect work to company objectives, track progress, monitor performance via real-time dashboards, reporting tools, and portfolios.

The Asana plans that include access to Goals & Reporting are Advanced+ plans for Goals, while Reporting is available on Starter+ plans.

Goals connect to projects and tasks by allowing you to link a goal to supporting projects, portfolios, or tasks, and Asana will update goal progress automatically based on that work.

You can set private versus public Goals in Asana. Goals can be shared with your entire organization or restricted to specific team members, allowing for public or private visibility.

Portfolios in Asana are tools that let you group multiple projects to monitor health and metrics across them with dashboards, custom charts, status updates, and workload views.

The plans that include Portfolios are Advanced, Enterprise, and Enterprise Plus plans.

Portfolios can contain other portfolios in Asana. You can nest portfolios so that portfolios can include other portfolios as well as projects.

Portfolio status updates are features that let you share performance summaries across projects in a portfolio using a built-in status builder.

Reporting dashboards in Asana are tools that let you visualize team and organizational data in real time using charts built from projects, portfolios, goals, or tasks.

The chart types available in dashboards include column, line, burnup, donut, number, and lollipop charts.

You can customize and share reporting dashboards in Asana. You can build custom charts, apply filters, and share dashboards with view-only access. You can also export dashboards to PDF or PPT.
